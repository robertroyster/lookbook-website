Common questions

Is this Google Analytics?

No. Google Analytics tracks page views and sessions. LookBook tracks menu behavior directly — which dishes get taps, which categories get browsed, and where traffic comes from. They're complementary.

Do I need to set up tags or UTMs?

No. LookBook has built-in attribution tracking. UTM parameters are supported but optional — tracking works out of the box.

Does this work for QR and Google?

Yes. Both QR scans and Google traffic are tracked as core sources, so you can see how guests arrive and what they browse.

Can I compare stores?

Yes. Multi-location brands can compare browsing patterns and top dishes across stores.

What about privacy?

LookBook tracks aggregated interaction signals — taps, browses, and sources. No personal data is collected or stored.
