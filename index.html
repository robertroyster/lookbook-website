Menu Visibility Score

Most menus are online. That doesn’t mean they’re visible.

Your menu might be on the internet, but can Google read it? Can AI recommend your dishes? Here’s how the most common formats actually score.

0

Flipbook / Image Viewer

A photo of a printed page. Google can’t read it. AI can’t parse it. Guests pinch-zoom and give up.

15

PDF Menu

Slightly better than a photo—but still a black hole for search. Hard to browse on a phone. Zero dish-level visibility.

45

Readable Web Menu

Text is visible to Google, but no photos, no structure for AI, and dishes don’t stand out. Better, but still weak.

100

Structured + Visual

Dish-level data Google can index. Photos that sell. Structure AI can recommend. This is what LookBook Menu builds.
