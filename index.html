Common questions

Is this online ordering?

No. LookbookMenu is menu enhancement—a visual browsing layer that helps guests choose faster and feel confident.

How fast can I see it?

In minutes. Send a PDF, a URL, or menu photos and we’ll generate a Try-It Lookbook immediately.

What if I don’t have great photos yet?

Start with the Try-It Lookbook, then use Photo Manager to swap in real dish photos as you shoot them—right from the line.

Can we do multiple menus (lunch/dinner/happy hour)?

Yes. Separate sections or separate menus—whatever matches how you serve.

Can it work for multi-location brands?

Yes. You can have a brand page plus location pages, with QRs for each.

Can guests view it at home before they come?

Yes. Share it anywhere—website, Google profile, Instagram bio, email.
