FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Is this online ordering?

No. LookBook is menu enhancement — not ordering. Guests browse your food visually, get hungry, and come in (or call). No commissions, no order management, no friction.

How fast can I see it?

Upload your menu and see it in 5 minutes. Your full production lookbook is typically live the same day.

What if I don't have great photos yet?

We source photos for you. Our photo manager pulls high-quality dish images automatically. You can replace them with your own any time — but you don't have to wait for a photoshoot to launch.

Can I do multiple menus (lunch/dinner/happy hour)?

Yes. Separate sections or separate menus — whatever matches how you serve.

Can this work for multiple locations?

Yes. Each location gets its own LookBook with its own analytics. You can manage all locations from one dashboard and push menu updates across the brand.

Can guests browse before they visit?

Yes — and that's the point. Guests browse your menu on Google, decide what they're craving, and show up ready to order. No app download required.

Can I use this for specials and events?

Yes. Specials are one of the strongest use cases. LookBook helps you highlight the dishes and drinks you most want to sell, with imagery that makes them easier to notice and crave.

Why is this better than a PDF?

A PDF lists your food. LookBook helps sell it. Instead of forcing guests to read through words, it helps them discover dishes visually, crave what they see, and choose with more confidence.

What is the ROI?

At $49/month, one extra table visit or one upsold dish per week more than covers it. Most restaurants see the difference in how guests order — more confidently, more premium items, fewer "what do you recommend?" questions.
