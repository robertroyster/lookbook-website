The LookBook path

LookBook creates deep links for every category and dish. Instead of a PDF, your Google profile links to real food pages that guests love to browse.

Deep links for everything

Every category and dish gets its own URL. Use them in your Google Business Profile menu section, Google Posts, event links, and more.
