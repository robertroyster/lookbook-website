Visual browsing, not reading.

Guests scroll through dish photos organized by category — appetizers, entrées, desserts, cocktails. Each dish shows a photo, name, description, and price. It feels like browsing a feed, not reading a document.

Category pages

Every menu section gets its own shareable page. Link directly to appetizers from your Google profile, or cocktails from an Instagram post.

  • /your-restaurant/category/appetizers
  • /your-restaurant/category/desserts
  • /your-restaurant/category/cocktails

Dish pages

Every dish gets its own page with a photo, description, price, and dietary tags. Share individual dishes in Google Posts, emails, or ads.

  • /your-restaurant/dish/buffalo-chicken-pizza
  • /your-restaurant/dish/chocolate-lava-cake

QR → phone → food

Guests scan a QR code at the table and instantly see your menu as a visual feed. No app download. No pinching a PDF. Just beautiful food on their phone.
